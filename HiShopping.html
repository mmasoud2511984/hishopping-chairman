Start Your Journey with HiShopping Today! Get 100 OMR Immediately Upon Registration

The application is a new payment method in the Sultanate of Oman that allows users to recover the value of their consumption bills in the form of points within the application. For example: If you purchase worth 100 Omani Riyals at any store or mall participating in the app, you will directly receive 100 points in the app (1 point = 1 Omani Riyal), and you can convert the points into cash balance through daily release.

Registration and Downloading the App
  1. First, scan the following code with your phone or click on the registration link instead of scanning the code, and you will be redirected directly to the registration page
  2. Fill in the fields as follows:
    1. Enter the name you want to appear in the app.
    2. Enter your mobile number in Oman.
    3. Click on the Octane Code button and you will receive an OTP code on your phone.
    4. Enter the OTP code sent to you in the Enter Code field.
    5. Enter a password for the app and confirm it.
    6. Click on Confirm Register.
  3. You will then be redirected directly to the app download page.
  4. Download the app according to your phone: iPhone via App Store or Android via Google Play.
  5. After downloading, open the app and allow notifications.
  6. Choose your preferred language within the app.
  7. Enable location if requested.
  8. Click on the "Accounts" button at the bottom and log in.
  9. Log in by selecting Login via phone number (not via email) and enter your mobile number and the password you previously registered, then click on login
  10. Your account has been successfully activated and you now have a balance of 100 points as a new user, which appears in the wallet
How to Add Balance to the App
  1. Open the app and click on the "Accounts" button at the bottom.
  2. Then click on Consumption Points.
  3. Then click on Recharge.
  4. Then click on Bank Transfer and choose the payment method that suits you.
  5. For example, if you want to transfer 300 Omani Riyals from your Bank Muscat account via mobile number: follow these steps:-
    1. Go to your bank app and through (Mobile Payment System) transfer 300 Omani Riyals - in the usual way in your bank - to the number shown in the HiShopping app and take a screenshot of the transfer details from your bank app
    2. Return to the HiShopping app and enter the amount 300 in the amount field and attach the screenshot you took from your bank account and click on the camera icon to attach it then click on confirm
  6. Wait a few minutes then go to the accounts screen and you will notice that the balance has been added to your Consumption Points and thus you have a full 300 Omani Riyals balance that you can use to purchase from any store participating with Hishopping
How to Make a Purchase
  1. Open the app and click on the scanner icon at the top of the main screen
  2. Scan the QR code of the merchant or store you want to purchase from, then enter the amount and confirm the transaction.
  3. You will notice after a successful payment that the amount is deducted from the wallet first, and if there is not enough balance there, it will be deducted from the Consumption Points.
  4. You will also notice that the value of the paid amount is added to the wallet.
How to Benefit from Points and Release Them
  1. Open the app and click on the wallet from the accounts page
  2. Click on the "Click to Release Points" button
  3. Then on the next page also click on the "Click to Release Points" button, noting that points can be released only once daily, and the release day is marked in green on the calendar.
  4. Wait until you finish watching the advertisement video, and thus you have released the points from the wallet to the Cash wallet.
  5. Note that the daily release percentage of points is as follows
    • Regular User: Points are released at 0.1%, meaning if you have 1000 points in the wallet, 1 Omani Riyal will be released on the first day, then decreases according to the remaining points.
    • Silver User: Points are released at 0.2%, meaning if you have 1000 points in the wallet, 2 Omani Riyals will be released on the first day, then decreases according to the remaining points.
    • Platinum User: Points are released at 0.6%, meaning if you have 1000 points in the wallet, 6 Omani Riyals will be released on the first day, then decreases according to the remaining points.
    • Gold User: Points are released at 0.7%, meaning if you have 1000 points in the wallet, 7 Omani Riyals will be released on the first day, then decreases according to the remaining points.
  6. You will notice that points are deducted from the wallet, and those released points are credited to the Cash wallet at 80% and to the wallet at 20%.
  7. After that, you can withdraw money from the Cash wallet via bank transfer, and you can benefit from the wallet by purchasing from participating stores.
Available Packages and Features of Each
  1. There are 3 packages available in addition to the regular user, which are as follows:
    • Regular User: This is the new user who does not have any package and has 100 points in the wallet automatically, with a daily release rate of 0.2%.
    • Silver Package: Obtained for a one-time payment of 10 Omani Riyals, with a daily release rate of 0.2%, and upon subscription, the user is granted 20 points in the wallet.
    • Platinum Package: Obtained for a one-time payment of 100 Omani Riyals, with a daily release rate of 0.6%, and upon subscription, the user is granted 300 points in the wallet.
    • Gold Package: Obtained for a one-time payment of 300 Omani Riyals, with a daily release rate of 0.7%, and upon subscription, the user is granted 1200 points in the wallet.
  2. VIP purchase is a temporary campaign launched by HiShopping starting on April 1, 2025, and the end date will be announced in the app system 30 days in advance.
  3. When the VIP account balance reaches zero, the release speed returns to the regular user speed (which is 0.1%).
How to Activate a Package
  1. Open the app and click on the "Accounts" button at the bottom.
  2. Click on Purchase Star Membership.
  3. Click again on Purchase Star Membership.
  4. Choose the package that suits you, making sure the amount is available in your Consumption Points.
  5. Agree to the terms and click on Confirm Payment.
  6. Congratulations, you have become a member according to the package you activated, and check the points added to your wallet.
How to Withdraw Cash Points
  1. Open the app and click on the "Accounts" button at the bottom.
  2. Click on the Cash Points wallet.
  3. Then click on the Withdraw button.
  4. Fill in your details carefully:
    1. Enter the name as it appears on your bank card
    2. Select your bank
    3. Enter your bank account number
    4. Enter the number of points you want to withdraw (each point = 1 Omani Riyal)
    5. Then click on the Confirm button
  5. Note: A 10% fee is deducted from the cash withdrawal amount.
How to Invite a Friend to Register in the App
  1. Open the app and click on the "Accounts" button at the bottom.
  2. Click on My Invitation.
  3. Then click on Invite Friends.
  4. Download your QR images or share them directly via one of the communication methods
  5. Ask your friend to scan the QR code and follow the same registration steps mentioned earlier.
  6. This way, the new user will be added directly under you, and you can benefit from them when they obtain the Gold Package.
